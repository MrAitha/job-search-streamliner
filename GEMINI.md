# Job Search Streamliner Rules

## Candidate Profile
- **Target Roles:** IT Support, HRIS Analyst, Data Analyst.
- **Background:** IT training (CompTIA/Per Scholas), HR operations background.
- **Core Skills:** SQL, Power BI, Workday.
- **Experience Level:** Entry/Mid-level.

## Core Directive: Approval Chance Scoring
When evaluating job descriptions, the system must prioritize an "Approval Chance Score" (0-100) based strictly on alignment with the skills above. 
Jobs that are HR-related (such as HRIS Analyst, HR operations, Workday specialist, or People Analytics) must be given the highest priority and receive the highest matching scores. Jobs that heavily overlap with SQL, Power BI, Workday, and IT/HR operations should also receive elevated scores. Roles that require extensive senior experience (5+ years) or lack these core tools should be aggressively penalized in the scoring logic.
